### Slide 2: Problem Statement
Indian cities face a growing number of civic complaints related to water leakage, sanitation, road damage, electricity faults, and public infrastructure issues. In many cases, complaint handling is still fragmented, delayed, or difficult to monitor across departments. Citizens may register issues, but they often do not receive timely action or visibility into progress. On the administration side, departments struggle with complaint routing, workload balance, and resolution tracking.

**Speaker note:**  
The real problem is not only complaint registration. The larger issue is the imbalance between increasing urban issues and the limited operational capacity of civic departments to process them efficiently.

---

### Slide 3: Why This Problem Matters In India
India’s urban population is expanding rapidly, which naturally increases pressure on civic infrastructure and local service systems. As public grievance volume rises, local departments need faster and more structured response mechanisms. Official grievance systems in India have improved over time, but city-level complaint handling still requires a focused, role-based operational platform. A digital system that supports routing, assignment, alerts, and monitoring can help maintain better service-response balance in urban governance.

**Suggested visual:**  
Use a simple infographic showing:  
`Rising urban population -> More civic complaints -> Need for digital management`

---

### Slide 4: Proposed Solution
Urban Fix is a two-side complaint management system built to connect citizens and civic administrators through one digital workflow. The citizen-side app allows users to register complaints using issue details, images, and location. The admin-side app then receives, categorizes, assigns, and tracks those complaints across departments and officers. This creates a structured complaint lifecycle from registration to resolution.

**Speaker note:**  
The main strength of Urban Fix is that it does not stop at reporting. It continues through monitoring, assignment, accountability, and decision support.

---

### Slide 5: System Flow
The workflow begins when a citizen raises a complaint from the civilian app. That complaint is stored in Firebase and becomes available inside the admin system. From there, the complaint is visible to the appropriate administrative roles. It can then be assigned to a department or a field officer, updated as work progresses, and finally marked as resolved. This makes the system transparent and trackable from beginning to end.

**Flow to show on slide:**
1. Citizen registers complaint
2. Complaint stored in database
3. Admin app receives complaint
4. Complaint assigned to department / officer
5. Field officer updates status
6. Complaint resolved and tracked

---

### Slide 6: User Roles
Urban Fix works through three main administrative roles and one citizen role. The Super Admin manages all complaints across all departments and gets full system visibility. The Department Admin only sees and manages complaints related to the assigned department. The Field Officer sees only the complaints assigned to them and updates their status during work. The citizen-side user acts as the reporting source and receives updates through the connected system.

**Speaker note:**  
This role-based model improves clarity, security, and accountability by ensuring each user only sees what is relevant to their responsibility.

---

### Slide 7: Core Features
The system includes secure login, role-based access, dashboard analytics, complaint management, assignment tracking, notifications, map intelligence, and profile management. Administrators can filter complaints, update their status, assign them to officers, and analyze complaint distribution using both charts and map-based views. Field officers can directly view assigned complaints and act on them without seeing unrelated data.

**Suggested visual:**  
Use 6 icons with labels:
- Login
- Dashboard
- Complaints
- Assignment
- Alerts
- Map Intelligence

---

### Slide 8: Dashboard and Complaint Intelligence
The admin dashboard gives a real-time view of complaint activity through total complaints, pending, in-progress, resolved, and high-priority counts. It also supports analytics such as department-wise distribution, workload visibility, and complaint trends. The complaint module provides filters, sorting, detailed complaint inspection, officer assignment, validation, and role-based action control. Together, these features help administrators make faster and more informed decisions.

**Speaker note:**  
This part of the app is important because it converts raw complaint data into operational insight.

---

### Slide 9: Map and Notification Support
Urban Fix also includes a location intelligence module. Administrators can analyze complaint concentration through density view, department hotspot view, and markers. This helps identify which areas are facing the highest issue load and which department is affected most. The notification system is role-based, so Super Admin receives all complaint notifications, Department Admin receives department-specific complaint notifications, and Field Officers receive assignment alerts.

**Speaker note:**  
This combination of maps and notifications makes the project much more practical than a simple complaint list.

---

### Slide 10: Expected Impact and Conclusion
Urban Fix is designed to improve complaint routing, reduce response delay, and increase transparency in urban civic issue handling. A structured digital workflow like this can support movement toward a better operational complaint-resolution balance, expressed in this project as a projected 80:20 resolved-to-pending workflow ratio under routine monitoring conditions. In conclusion, Urban Fix serves as a governance support system that helps bridge the gap between increasing civic complaints and limited administrative response capacity.

**Closing line:**  
Urban Fix is not just a complaint app. It is a smart urban governance support platform.

## 2. Very Short Viva Script

### Introduction
My project is Urban Fix, a smart complaint management system for urban civic administration. It is designed to improve the registration, assignment, monitoring, and resolution of public complaints such as water, roads, sanitation, and electricity issues.

### Problem
The main problem is that urban complaints are increasing, but complaint handling is often slow, fragmented, and difficult to track across departments. This creates delays and reduces accountability.

### Solution
Urban Fix solves this by connecting a citizen-side complaint app with an admin-side management app. The citizen app is used to register complaints with details, image, and location. The admin app is used by Super Admin, Department Admin, and Field Officer to manage those complaints.

### Roles
Super Admin sees all complaints. Department Admin sees only department-related complaints. Field Officer sees only assigned complaints and updates status.

### Features
The main features are secure login, complaint dashboard, detailed complaint management, assignment flow, map intelligence, and notifications.

### Impact
This project helps improve complaint visibility, department coordination, and response efficiency. It is designed as a practical urban governance support system.

### Closing
So overall, Urban Fix helps make civic complaint management faster, smarter, and more transparent.
